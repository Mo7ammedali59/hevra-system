Privacy Policy

At Hevra+, your privacy is very important to us. This page outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal data such as your name, email, phone number, gender, role, medical condition, and login credentials when you register on our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized healthcare services.
  • To improve your user experience and suggestions.
  • To contact you regarding appointments, reminders, or updates.

3. Data Protection

We use secure servers, encryption, and access control mechanisms to ensure the confidentiality of your data.

4. Sharing Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. It may be shared with healthcare professionals only for medical purposes and with your consent.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your information at any time. Contact our support team for assistance.

6. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. All updates will be posted on this page with the effective date.
