Doctor Privacy Policy

At Hevra+, we respect the privacy and data security of all medical professionals using our platform. This Doctor Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information of doctors who use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Name, contact information, and professional credentials
  • Medical license and verification documents
  • Consultation records and availability schedules
  • Device and usage information from our app/website

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Verify your identity and medical credentials
  • Enable communication between doctors and patients
  • Improve our services and platform functionality
  • Comply with legal obligations and medical standards

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share it with:

  • Healthcare authorities and regulators when required
  • Third-party service providers who support our platform
  • Patients with whom you have active consultations

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at privacy@hevra.com for any requests or concerns.

6. Updates to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. We will notify users of any major changes through our website or email.
